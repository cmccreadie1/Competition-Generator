FISHER-YATES RANDOMIZATION

This tournament uses the mathematical gold standard for unbiased shuffling. The probability of any angler drawing a specific peg is exactly:

$$P(x) = \frac{1}{n!}$$

It is the same logic used in professional gaming and high-security encryption. Once the shuffle begins, the result is locked and unalterable.
